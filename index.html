Our strategy

Our strategy

Innovation and sustainability. The two go hand in hand for us and provide firm foundations for our business strategy. Our new products and services always start with a focus on sustainability. Making a positive impact on our local communities and the planet while growing the company creates value for us and our stakeholders around the world.

We're going for growth

Our universal strategy for growth unifies us as a global business. It’s what differentiates us from other companies, and it embodies how we benefit our communities and stakeholders, wherever we operate. Solving customer problems is what drives us to innovate. We focus on finding solutions to real industry challenges in creative, sustainable ways, so that we grow through the new markets we create. It’s how we put our commitment towards a better world into practice every day.

A more sustainable future

Having high standards on environmental, social and governance (ESG) issues is not only the right thing to do, it makes us stronger as a brand and promotes sustainable, long-term growth. By 2050, our target is to achieve net zero waste and 100 per cent renewable energy usage in our day-to-day operations. We also aim to reduce greenhouse gas emissions through installing energy-efficient products in 60 million homes worldwide. That’s an ambitious agenda for a building products company, and it will require our entire team’s commitment to make it into a reality. We can’t think of a better place to put our energy.
